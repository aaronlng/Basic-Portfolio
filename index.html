About Me

Aaron Long was grew up in the Lehigh Valley area. He went to high school at Southern Lehigh High School and graduated in 2010. While in high school he was a part of the band and orchestra, participated in the school musical, and also played on the baseball team.


In the fall of 2010 he began attending American University in Washington, DC, studying audio production and music. While at the university, he played trumpet in the symphonic band and orchestra. He also participated in the club baseball team throughout his tenure. He graduated in 2014 with a Bachelors of Arts in Music and Audio Production.
